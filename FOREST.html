FOREST DAYDREAM

Installation art exhibition at Wellcome Collection, London

Wellcome Collection X UAL's Creative Computing Institute

The installation consists of luminous forest and maze, in which parents and children can immerse themselves to explore the changes of light and shadow.

The Objective

The project aims to build a digital forest in the noisy city, so that the audience can relax in immersion. There are many different modes of lighting and music to show different weather. The audience can trigger the change of weather mode by trying to press the button on the "tree".

Role: Tree part
Agency: Wellcome Collection
Date: Spring.2020
