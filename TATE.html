UNI TO UNICORNS

Installation art exhibition at Tate Modern, London

Tate Modern X Digital Maker Collective

The Digital Maker Collective is a volunteer led, informal Research & Development (R&D) group, made up of University of the Arts London UAL staff, students, alumni who share common goals in exploring digital & emerging technologies in the context of arts practice, education, society and the creative industries.

Uni to Unicorns was not only an art exhibition, but also an event provide opportunities for members to communicate.

The project on exhibition

The project was an interactive audio-visual installation. The player could control the change of light and animations on screen through keyboard.

Role: Member
Agency: Tate Modern
